Gummy Hearts

Gummy hearts are chewy, heart-shaped candies often found in pick-and-mix selections or sold for Valentine's Day.

They typically have a fruity flavor, like strawberry, and may feature a soft foam bottom for added texture. Some variations include multiple layers or a sanded sugar coating.
